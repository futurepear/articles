Update 2.7 has arrived!

Soon, the old pre-2.0 map, "exodus", will be returning to realmz.io. However, there will be significant changes to accommodate for the map's return. This map has been tried and tested and is ideal for PvP.

The map Exodus

The map is undergoing a remaster which will include most of the current biomes in the game and new decorations.

Temporarily Removing Bosses

We decided that in the current state that advanced enemies and bosses retract from the realmz.io PvP experience. Bosses will be removed from the game for a short period of time until they will be added back in seperate worlds. Bosses will also have a cooldown timer.

Advanced Enemies that will be unavaliable:
  • Goblins
  • Goblin Warlock
  • Ancient Statues
  • Erthium Eye
  • Mechanical Terror
  • Castaway

When?

Unfortunately our development process is slow and we do not have any set release date.
