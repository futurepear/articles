In this article I will be teaching you how to make the famous "Gatorade Bread"

For ingredients, you will need

  • Two cups of flour
  • Table of dry yeast
  • 1 Cup of lukewarm gatorade
  • Tablespoon of sugar
  • Tablespoon of butter

    • Instructions

    Melt your better, then put it into the gatorade.

    Then, mix yeast into it and let it sit until it bubbles.

    In a large bowl, put the two cups of flour, and dump the yeast-gatorade mixture in.

    If you need to, add extra gatoraid slowly while stirring in circular motion until dough starts to form into ball.

    Knead the dough and sprinkle flour on it occasionally.

    Shape dough how you want it to be. Let rise for 20 mins to 30 minutes.

    Preheat your oven to 350 degrees and put your gatorade bread in.
