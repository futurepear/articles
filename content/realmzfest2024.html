Realmzfest 2024


The Official Realmz.io 1v1 tournament


FuturePearStudios is hosting Realmzfest 2024 - the largest realmz.io tournament. Tournament prizes include over $50, new special skins, coins, and more! Anyone with a realmz.io account can sign up. If you have questions, email futurepearstudios@gmail.com

After the deadline for signing up, players will be organized into a bracket. Players will have 3 1v1s with the same weapon, and whoever wins moves on up the bracket. Bracket information will be posted in April on this page.
