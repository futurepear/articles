Instructions once you join

1. Log into your realmz.io account

2. Set your username to your account username for the checkmark next to your name

3. Join the lobby and ask a Judge or Host to begin your match. You may have to wait




Round 2 Timeline:

April 13-14: SECOND ROUND OF TOURNAMENTS

You can show up at ANY TIME during tournament times on April 13-14 to have your match. You fight against a random person who is online at the same time. Your match will take 20 minutes at most.

To join the Arena server, use the server dropdown menu or use a link that will be posted in this news article on April 13-14.


IMPORTANT: Time per day has been cut in half, see table below.

April 13 April 14

Please note that the times are in the Central Time Zone. Please convert them to your time zone to make sure you will be on time.

Makeup Rounds

We are NOT using submission from the makeup form because it is impossible to make time for random rounds. If you missed round 1, show up to round 2 WITH someone else who missed their round 1 match.



Weapons:

Both players must agree to use the same weapon for each round, or else a judge will decide. You can request other weapons if both players agree. You will have 3 rounds against the other player.

Default allowed weapons:
  • Paladin/Champion
  • Sniper
  • Rune Wizard & Cyber Blaster
  • Pulse Bow
  • Spear/Spork


Prizes:

If you reach a bracket, you will win the following prizes: (This is subject to change). Prizes do not stack.
  • Winner: $50, 100000 stars, 10000 coins, 20 erthium, some special skins, tournament winner badge
  • Finalist: 1 special skin, 10 erthium, 50000 stars, 5000 coins
  • Semifinalists: 5 erthium, 10000 stars, 1000 coins
  • Pre-semifinalists (8 total): 500 coins, 1 erthium
